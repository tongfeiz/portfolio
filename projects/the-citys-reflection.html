"The City's Reflection"

Light projection in Hong Kong, exploring the relationship between urban architecture and natural light.

Social media is becoming more integral in our lives, but our choices are being influenced, and our behavior is being manipulated. Our freedom is disappearing, and we are slowly losing touch with our own true identity. I created a piece of digital art inspired by my wall painting and projected it around my city in Hong Kong, sparking reflection and contemplation.
