Overview

As I grew up,I felt like I was constantly being controlled and monitored by the world around me. My parents, teachers, peers... This piece is one of my first digital art pieces. It really defined my art style, finding balance and harmony in the chaos. I later turned this piece into a mural in Hong Kong.
