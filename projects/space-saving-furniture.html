Space-Saving Furniture

Chairs designed specifically for small living quarters, maximizing comfort while minimizing space usage.

Role

Product Designer

Team

Solo Project

Scope

March - May 2022

Tools

Rhino 3D

Adobe Photoshop

Procreate

Being sedentary in the same position for long hours can result in fatigue in many areas of the body. My multi-purpose furniture can change the form, encouraging the user to sit in a variety of positions. The intuitiveness and customizability of the furniture reduce the soreness of the body and elevate liveliness in a tight living space.
