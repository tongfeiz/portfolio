Overview

I painted a mural at Ningbo's Mondiland Suit Factory. This was the tallest mural I have ever painted. The factory provided me with a basic scaffolding, and I was able to paint the mural in a few days, but it was extremely challenging, especially when I had to move around on the unstable scaffolding. I almost fell off... several times...

Reflection

Honestly I've been thinking long and hard for a title but I still haven't come up with one... For now, it'll remain "Untitled", like many other projects I've started but didn't have time to finish.
